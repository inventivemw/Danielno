Title: Northside

----

Text: Northside is a Melbourne tour guide highlighting the experience in Northcote, Collingwood, Fitzroy and Carlton. Traversing through the bars, restaurants, parks, shops and plenty more.
The design follows a Swiss approach, drawing influences from German typographer and graphic designer, Otl Aicher, and his work on Lufthansa and the 1972 Munich Olympics
